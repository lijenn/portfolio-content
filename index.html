Hime (she/her) here!

I was then nursed back to health and have been living as an indoor cat since then. My birthday is March 28, 2021 and I'm a domestic short- haired tabby cat. I weigh 10 lbs and enjoy nomming on dry kibble, prescription wet food (I get tummy aches from eating too fast), and wet treats.

Though I'm a relatively chill girl, I'm still a bit feral. When I get excited, I like to bite fingers and feet (especially Jennifer's). Otherwise, give me a comfy cat bed next to a big window and I'm all set! My favorite activity (besides napping and destroying things) is chasing a red laser and zooming around the house. I enjoy the occasional petting, but only for a few minutes when you wake me up from my nap. I'm independent so I would not prefer to have a sibling, but I can tolerate them if you give me enough time.
