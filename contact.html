Contact Information

While my owner is busy with graduate school and life, sometimes she needs support in taking care of me while she is out getting the self-care that she needs.
For example, she has the following dates planned for travel: 12/20-12/31

If you’re a cat-lover that’s okay with stopping by to refill my water bowl, give me a treat or two, and refill my automatic cat feeder until it is full, I’ll be all set!
If you’re interested in being my short-term human (volunteer or paid – we can discuss further details over a few treats!), then fill out the form with your name, contact information, a time that works for you to talk about details!

Before you contact us, here’s what we’re looking for in a human:
  • Is experienced with cat-sitting for at least 1 year
  • Has experience with cats who are/act semi-feral
  • Has availability to check-in on cat at least 2x a day
  • Has references who will vouch for your cat-sitting experience
  • A good communicator who will respond to requests or questions within a reasonable timeframe
  • Has a driver’s license and car, in case I run out of food (or I tear through my kibble bag again and you’ll need to get a container)
  • Is patient with me as I can be very stubborn

We will do our best to respond within one or two days!
Hope we’re a good fit for each other :)
