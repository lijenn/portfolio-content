Care Instructions

Daily Feeding Schedule:
- Breakfast (7:30AM) - less than 1/4 cup of Dry Kibble
- Lunch - (Noon - 1:00PM) - 2 spoonfuls of Prescription Wet Food
- Dinner - (6:00PM) - less than 1/4 cup of Dry Kibble
- Treats - (As needed) - 1-2 Greenies treats
- Water - (As needed) - Refill water bowl

Flea Medication Schedule:
- Apply my flea medication (Cheristine) on the 28th day of every month.
- Move the fur on the back of neck and apply the topical directly to my skin.
