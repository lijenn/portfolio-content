Care Instructions

My daily feeding schedule is quite simple! As a reminder, my type of species thrives off of a scheduled routine, so please set alarms and/or reminders to ensure my schedule is followed to a tea (Do not worry if you can’t, as I will adapt. But I prefer we stick on schedule)! My daily feeding schedule is as follow:

  • Breakfast starts at 7:30AM - less than 1/4 cup of Dry Kibble
  • Lunch starts from Noon - 1:00PM - 2 spoonfulls of Hill’s I/D Prescription Wet Food
  • Dinner starts at 6:00PM - less than 1/4 cup of Dry Kibble
  • Treats are on an as needed basis (hint: ask me to sit!) - 1-2 Greenies treats or Churu wet treats
  • Water is on an as needed basis - Refill water bowl

Other parts of my schedule include my medications and play-time! Since I am a cat, I also am prone to getting flea :( Please ensure I stay health by following my flea medication schedule as followed:

  • Apply my flea medication (Cheristine) on the 28th day of every month.
  • Move the fur on the back of neck and apply the topical directly to my skin.
    • My play scheduled is also to be followed to a tea! Otherwise, I will be prone to nibbling on your hands or feet since I’m itching to play. Play schedule:
  • Laser toy – for when I can’t stop nibbling on you and need a distraction
  • Tunnel – throw a catnip toy in there and I’ll chase it!
  • Bacon kicker toy – throw and watch me bite and rabbit-kick it till I run out of energy
